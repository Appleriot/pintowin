A youth program located at Northen High School. Leaded by Coach Demario.

Coach Demario Leathers is dedicated to empowering our youth with confidence, endurance, and discipline. Coach Leathers and other dedicated members are continuing the Leathers family legacy of teaching and training young athletes in the fundamentals of wrestling with an emphasis on preparing them for the next level of competition in middle school, high school, and college. Our club is comprised of a team of beginner and experienced wrestlers. Pin To Win is committed to the development and empowerment of future leaders and athletes by using wrestling as a tool to improve health and fitness, develop life skills and values, and become well-rounded, productive citizens..

Training Sessions

Team Practice takes place every Tuesday and Thursday from 6-7 pm. Team positions are available for ages 5 and up.

One Sport Fits All

Wrestling is the oldest sport known to man. Wrestling is for ALL people (tall, short, big, small, male, female, fast, slow, etc.). The Pin To Win Wrestling Program is here to help all inspired wrestling athletes, coaches, referees, managers, and parents reach their fullest possible potential in life. As our mascot, The Student Athlete, symbolizes the two most important areas in the Pin To Win Wrestling Program and that is to EDUCATE AND TRAIN. The Pin To Win staff is very dedicated to the upkeep of our teammates in-class behavior and performance as well as the sportsmanship, attitude, and success during competitions and other P2W events. The P2W wrestling curriculum is broken into 3 phases. This brilliant three phase curriculum will simply educate our teammates from the well known basics of wrestling to the smartest and trickiest wrestling maneuvers, strategies, and techniques.

Affiliates & Sponsors

There are many benefits to becoming a sponsor of the Pin To Win Wrestling Club. The P2W sponsorship lasts for one full year beginning on August 1st of every year. ALL sponsors receive online recognition. This includes but is not limited to Facebook and website sponsor pages. All sponsors will also be recognized on our yearly Pin To Win sponsorship poster. We also have promotional packages that include recognition on various t-shirts, flyers, posters, and the Pin To Win Wrestling Club banner. We use various flyers and posters to promote our events, but the Pin To Win Wrestling Club banner along with our various P2W t-shirts are advertised at ALL events that we host and attend.

  • Gold

    Online Recognition, Recognition on ALL posters, banners, flyers, and t-shirts

  • Silver

    Online Recognition, Recognition on sponsorship t-shirt and poster, Recognition on Pin To Win Wrestling Club banner

  • Bronze

    Online Recognition, Recognition on sponsorship poster
